
The Precion Solar System:

 - The central star is named Solaris.
 - Closest to Solaris is a lava-like planet called Utumu (contains the material Exotill).
 - Next to the planet utumu in a habitable zone is Nuew (a very Earth-like planet with a massive jungle).
 - Next to the planet Nuew is Polaria also in the habitable zone (a much colder planet with a massive mountain).
 - Next to the planet Polaria is Oblivio a waste world far from the central star (contains the material Bosnu).


Epoch 1 (Story):

Takes place on the planet Nuew in the small village on Takar.
